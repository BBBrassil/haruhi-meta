Methodology!!!!

How the Metachronological Order Works

"The Melancholy of Haruhi Suzumiya"

  1. The first school day after Golden Week…

    Haruhi is inspired to form the SOS Brigade in the beginning of May.

  2. The next Tuesday—Thursday…

    Part 2 takes place over the course of three school days as Haruhi acquires resources for the club.

  3. Friday—Monday…

    In Part 3, Haruhi announces that the SOS Brigade's first city-wide search will commence on the upcoming Sunday. Kyon receives a note from Ryoko Asakura on the Monday after.

  4. Tuesday—Thursday…

    Part 4 takes place on the day after Kyon's talk with Ryoko Asakura.

"The Boredom of Haruhi Suzumiya"

The second Sunday in June…

The SOS Brigade club members compete in a baseball tournament which is held on a Sunday in June.

"Bamboo Leaf Rhapsody"

July 7th…

This episode is set on Tanabata, which is celebrated on July 7th in Japan.

"Mystérique Sign"

The second Thursday in July…

This episode begins on the day after the first trimester final exams.

"Remote Island Syndrome"

The third weekend in July…

The SOS Brigade takes an overnight trip at the start of summer vacation.

"Endless Eight"

August…

In the "Endless Eight" arc, we see the rest of the SOS Brigade's summer vacation.

The most immersive watching experience goes:

  1. On August 1st, Part 1.
  2. From August 2nd to August 30th, a random selection from Parts 2—6.
  3. On August 31st, Part 8.

If you don't want to repeat episodes, you can watch only the first occurrence of each part. They will be in order and spread throughout the month.

…or, you could watch only Parts 1 & 8. I won't judge!

Cultural Festival

  1. A week and a half before the festival…

    In "The Sigh of Haruhi Suzumiya", the club members film an SOS Brigade original movie to screen at the school's cultural festival. They work hard to complete the movie on time, filming all day Saturday and Sunday.

  2. The Saturday nearest November 3rd…

    "Live Alive" and "The Adventures of Mikuru Asahina Episode 00" both take place on the day of the festival. We get to watch two episodes today!

  3. The next Wednesday…

    "Day of Sagittarius" begins a few days after the festival.

"Someday in the Rain"

The last Friday in November…

This anime-original episode is set sometime around the beginning of winter.

The Disappearance of Haruhi Suzumiya

December 16—21…

The movie is set between December 16th and December 21st, with most of the action occurring on the 18th.
